MBA is a non-profit Scholarship Foundation dedicated to empowering less-privileged individuals through scholarships, educational grants, mentorship programs, and skill development initiatives. Our mission is to break the cycle of poverty by ensuring equal access to quality education, enabling young minds to unlock their full potential and contribute to society.

Vision

To create a world where every child, regardless of socioeconomic background, has access to quality education, empowering them to become leaders, innovators, and changemakers in their communities and beyond.
