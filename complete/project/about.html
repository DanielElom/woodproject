Wood & Steel Medallions Nigeria Limited Founded in 2018 and officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in 2020, Wood & Steel Medallions Nigeria Limited is a leading manufacturing and steel fabrication company in Nigeria, committed to bridging the gap between aesthetics, innovation, and cost-effectiveness. We specialize in delivering premium interior, residential, and commercial fabrication solutions that combine quality craftsmanship, innovative design, and advanced technology. Our reputation is built on trust, reliability, and value-driven results, making us a top choice for clients who demand excellence in manufacturing and fabrication services in Nigeria. Driven by innovation and bold risk-taking, we continuously research, develop, and adopt new technologies that meet today's needs while preparing for tomorrow's possibilities. .

Vision

Our vision at Wood & Steel Medallions Nigeria Limited is to become the most trusted brand in Nigeria's manufacturing and fabrication industry, recognized for our skilled professionals, innovative solutions, and cutting-edge technology. We aim to be the leading provider of interior and commercial fabrication solutions for both residential and corporate projects, consistently delivering excellence, customer satisfaction, and industry-leading innovation..
