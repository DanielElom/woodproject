About Us

Wood & Steel Medallions Nigeria Limited
Founded in 2018 and officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in 2020.

Wood & Steel Medallions Nigeria Limited is a leading manufacturing and steel fabrication company in Nigeria, committed to bridging the gap between aesthetics, innovation, and cost-effectiveness.
We specialize in delivering premium interior, residential, and commercial fabrication solutions that combine quality craftsmanship, innovative design, and advanced technology
.Our reputation is built on trust, reliability, and value-driven results, making us a top choice for clients who demand excellence in manufacturing and fabrication services in Nigeria.
Driven by innovation and bold risk-taking, we continuously research, develop, and adopt new technologies that meet today's needs while preparing for tomorrow's possibilities.
